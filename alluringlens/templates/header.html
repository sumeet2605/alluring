Akanksha Rajput

Akanksha Rajput is a maternity, newborn, baby photographer and founder at Alluring lens by AR based in Banglore.
Photography has been a part of Akanksha’s life since the day she was given her first camera, a Nikon D3500, almost 10 years ago. The professional bent came when she became a mom in 2019-20.

Akanksha is involved in all the aspects of her work – from creating backdrops, weaving props, designing clothes, styling, post-processing and everything in-between.
She considers her design sense and attention to detail as her most valuable assets. Akanksha lives with her husband and is a mom to a 4 year old daughter. Akanksha has studied Information Technology in Bhiwani and completed Diploma in Photography from Drishti School of Photography and National Institute of Creative Communication, Banglore

She is an ex-software professional, who quit her Software career to pursue her passions and to take care of a naughty 4 year old. She is a mom first and photographer next. So be rest assured that your baby is safe in the hands of another mother!

Anchal Rajput

Anchal is a co-founder at Alluring lens by AR based in Banglore.
Anchal is involved in all the aspects of her work – from handeling Social Media, marketing, designing clothes, styling, post-processing and everything in-between.
She considers her design sense and attention to detail as her most valuable assets.

Anchal has completed B.Sc. in Biotech from Jain University, Banglore and then has learned Makeup and UI/UX design from National Institute of Creative Communication, Banglore.

Anchal also becomes a model sometimes when a new concept needs to be executed.
Her passion is to take Alluring Lens Studio to the next level.
